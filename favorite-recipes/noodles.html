Ramen Egg Drop

Make some instant ramen. Slide an egg into the hot broth, then some butter.
Crown the steaming noodles with slices of American cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack ramen noodles with flavor packet
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon butter
  • 2 slices American cheese
  • ½ thinly sliced greens, optional

Steps

  1. Bring 2 1/2 cups of water to a boil in a small saucepan.
    Add the noodles and cook for 2 minutes.
    Add the flavor packet, stir, and continue to cook for another 30 seconds.
  2. Remove the pan from the heat and carefully add the egg. Do not stir;
    pull the noodles over the egg and let sit for one minute to poach.
  3. Carefully transfer everything to a serving bowl, add the butter, cheese and mix.
    Garnish with greens if desired.
